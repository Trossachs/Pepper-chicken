Our Nigerian Culinary Story

Founded in 2010, Pepper Chicken brings the vibrant and diverse flavors of Nigerian cuisine to your table. Our recipes have been passed down through generations, preserving the authentic tastes and traditional cooking methods that make Nigerian food so special.

We source our ingredients from local farmers and use traditional cooking techniques to ensure every dish bursts with authentic flavors. Our chefs, born and raised in different regions of Nigeria, bring their unique regional expertise to create a diverse menu that showcases the country's rich culinary heritage.

Our Journey

From a small family kitchen to a beloved restaurant

2010

The Beginning

Pepper Chicken started as a small family-owned food stall selling homemade Nigerian dishes.

2012

First Restaurant

We opened our first small restaurant with just 10 tables, serving a limited but authentic menu.

2015

Growth & Recognition

Pepper Chicken won its first culinary award and expanded to a larger location to meet growing demand.

2018

Culinary Excellence

Chef Oluwaseun joined as Executive Chef, enhancing our menu with regional specialties.

2020

Celebrating 10 Years

We celebrated a decade of serving authentic Nigerian cuisine and launched our catering services.

Today

Continuing Our Journey

We continue to grow while staying true to our mission of sharing authentic Nigerian flavors with the world.
